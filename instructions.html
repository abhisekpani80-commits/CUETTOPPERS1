📋 GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS FOR CANDIDATES

GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Total duration of the examination is 60 minutes.
  2. The clock will be set at the server. The countdown timer at the top right corner of your screen will display the remaining time available for you to complete the exam. When the timer reaches zero, the examination will end by itself. You need not terminate the exam or submit your paper.
  3. The Question Palette displayed on the right side of screen will show the status of each question using one of the following symbols:
    Grey — You have NOT visited the question yet.
    Red — You have NOT answered the question (but visited it).
    Green — You have answered the question.
    Purple — You have NOT answered the question but marked it for review.
    Purple + Green Dot — You have answered and also marked for review. Answer WILL be considered for evaluation.
  4. To select your answer, click on the button of one of the options.
  5. To deselect your chosen answer, click on the button of your chosen option again or click on the "Clear Response" button.
  6. To change your chosen answer, click the button of another option.
  7. To save your answer, you MUST click the "Save & Next" button.
  8. To mark a question for review, click the "Mark for Review & Next" button. If an answer is selected, it will be saved and considered for evaluation.
  9. To change your answer to a question, first select the question and then follow the procedure for answering that question.
  10. Note: Questions that are saved or answered and marked for review will ONLY be considered for evaluation.

NAVIGATING TO A QUESTION

  • Click on the question number in the Question Palette to go directly to that question. NOTE: This does NOT save your answer to the current question.
  • Click "Save & Next" to save your answer and proceed to the next question.
  • Click "Mark for Review & Next" to mark the current question and proceed to the next question.

SECTION NAVIGATION

  • Sections of this question paper are displayed on the top bar of the screen.
  • Questions in a section can be viewed by clicking on the section name.
  • The section you are currently viewing is highlighted.
  • You can shuffle between sections and questions anytime during the exam.

MARKING SCHEME — CUET (UG)

Situation Marks Details
Correct Answer +5 5 marks awarded for each correct answer
Wrong Answer −1 1 mark deducted for each incorrect answer
Unattempted 0 No deduction for skipped questions
Attempt Limit — Attempt any 40 of 50 questions in this test

ACCESSIBILITY FEATURES

  • Font size can be toggled using A− / A / A+ buttons in the top bar.
  • Keyboard navigation: use Tab to move between options, Enter to select.
  • Your progress is auto-saved every 30 seconds to prevent data loss.
  • If your browser refreshes during the exam, you will be restored to exactly where you left off.

IMPORTANT WARNINGS

  • Do not press the browser's Back button during the examination.
  • Do not open this exam in multiple tabs — it will invalidate your session.
  • Ensure a stable internet connection throughout the examination.
  • This is an UNOFFICIAL mock test — scores hold no academic value.
